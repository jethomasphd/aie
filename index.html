The audience everyone's trying to simulate.
We have the humans.

Results Generation runs job boards across 17 countries. Each day, 25M emails move 3.2M job seekers into live search sessions, and 1.5M of them take a real action — an application, a registration. No one channels a person from inbox to outcome the way we do. We're opening that funnel to AI products that need real humans, fast: for activation, for placement, and for the human data simulated agents can't supply.

Reach

In-search ad units

Native units inside live job-search results — our highest-intent moment. Drive users straight to your page to sign up, engage, or apply, customized by keyword, traffic source, and creative across flexible mediums and volume. The engine that moves users at scale.

Reach

Direct email

Sponsored modules and dedicated sends through deliverability-engineered lifecycle programs — placement where inboxing actually happens, segmented to the seekers your product serves.

Premium

SMS & direct mail

When a moment deserves more than an impression. SMS reaches the device with near-universal open rates; addressed direct mail lands physically, impossible to scroll past. Higher-attention placements for partners who want depth, not just reach.

Data

Panels, studies & human data

Opt-in cohorts of real job seekers for user research, beta access, human trials, and preference / eval data — recruitable by archetype, measurable against real outcomes. Simulated agents are infinite; humans in transition are scarce. We have them at volume.

Also available · Performance pilots — pay per activated user (CPL / CPA) when a specific campaign calls for it, measured against your activation event.
Why this audience, why now

Scarce supply. Anyone can spin up simulated agents; real humans in transition are the bottleneck — and we channel them at a scale almost no one can match.

Decision density. In one season a person re-chooses budgeting, coverage, skills, daily tools, and routines. Your category is on that list.

Receptivity. New-tool adoption peaks during transitions — you skip the habit-inertia tax that eats most growth spend.
